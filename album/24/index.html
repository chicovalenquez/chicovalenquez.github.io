About Technology
This was the final EP of the trilogy recorded by the band in 2005. Petony tackles such topical technology issues as cloning, cybernetic organisms and shitty weathermen. Unlike the other 2 EPs, which were written and recorded live off the floor at Dadmobile HQ, Technology was an online collaboration between Pete and Tony that was actually written shortly after the release of Petopia.
