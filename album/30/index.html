About Live At Dadmobile Xmas Party
RCK performed at the Christmas Party along with various permutations of most of the other bands on Dadmobile at the time. RCK put in the only performance worth remembering. We had tried recording some of these tracks in studio earlier in the year but for whatever reason things kept getting delayed and eventually got lost in the great hard drive crash of 2005. As a result, this live recording ended up being the closest thing to an album RCK ever put out. The unnecessary swearing at the end of the last track is in response to a giant nail that had somehow gotten on stage and stabbed Greg in the foot.
